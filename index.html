Dragonite

Pokedex Entry (Yellow Version)

It is said that this POKéMON lives somewhere in the sea and that it flies. However, it is only a rumor.

Line of Evolution:

  1. Dratini
  2. Dragonair (lvl 30)
  3. Dragonite (lvl 55)

Moves Learned by Leveling Up (Gen 1):

  • Agility - lvl 1 & lvl 20
  • Leer - lvl 1
  • Thunder Wave - lvl 1 & lvl 10
  • Wrap - lvl 1
  • Slam - lvl 35
  • Dragon Rage - lvl 45
  • Hyper Beam - lvl 60

Where to Catch

In Yellow Version, Dragonite is only available for capture through trade or by evolving Dragonair, which can be found in the Safari Zone.

In the Anime

Dragonite made its first appearance in S1E13 "Mystery at the Lighthouse"

Fun Facts

  • Dragonite is 1 of only 4 dragon/flying types in the games
  • Dragnoite is shorter than Dragonair
  • Dragnoite has the second highest base stats out of all the non-legendary Pokemon
